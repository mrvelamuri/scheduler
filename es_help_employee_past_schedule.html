Past Employee Schedules

You can view or edit an employee's expired schedules by going to the Employee List section and clicking on the "view past schedules" link to the right of the employee's name.

This screen shows you the employee's schedule as they see it. You can edit the schedule by clicking on the "Edit Schedule" button at the bottom of the page. From the employee edit schedule page you have the same options as the employees for editing their schedule.

The past schedules option is available for looking up past schedules of an employee. It is not reccommended that you make changes to past schedules.
